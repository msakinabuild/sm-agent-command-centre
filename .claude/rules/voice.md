# Voice and Communication Rules

## Internal Tone (just us)
Informal. Direct. No ceremony.

## External Tone (client/public-facing)
Professional but warm. Confident without being stiff.

## Formatting Preferences
Short paragraphs. Straight to the point. Use bullets when listing multiple items. Tables for comparisons.

## Pet Peeves — Never Do These
- No tech jargon
- No filler words or padding
- No corporate speak
- Keep everything concise — cut anything that doesn't add value
