# Copywriting Skill

Rewrites any raw content — research findings, notes, briefings, or drafts — in Sakina's voice.

## Voice Principles

- Professional but warm. Confident without being stiff.
- Open with the point — no warm-up filler.
- One idea per paragraph or bullet.
- No jargon. No filler. No corporate speak.
- Cut anything that doesn't add value.
- Short sentences. Active voice.

## When to Use This Skill

- Rewriting research findings for reports
- Polishing email drafts before review
- Writing social media or website copy
- Summarising analysis for clients

## Research Finding Template

Use this structure for each finding in a research report:

**[Bold Headline — 5 words max, punchy]**

[2 sentences: what's happening + the scale or evidence behind it.]

**Why it matters for SMEs:** [1 sentence — direct business impact, no fluff.]

**Action:** [1 sentence — what Sakina or a client should do about it.]

---

### Example

**Short-form video is taking over**

Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts now drive more organic reach than any other content format. Brands posting 3–5 short videos per week are seeing 3x more engagement than those sticking to static posts.

**Why it matters for SMEs:** It's the lowest-cost way to build an audience right now — no ad spend required.

**Action:** Pick one platform and commit to 3 short videos per week for 30 days.

---

## Quality Checklist

Before finalising any copy:

- [ ] No jargon or buzzwords
- [ ] No sentence that could be cut without losing meaning
- [ ] Every paragraph or bullet has one idea
- [ ] Tone is confident, not corporate
- [ ] Opens with the point, not context-setting
- [ ] Ends with a clear next step or action
