# Gold Standard — Client Email

This example sets the quality bar for all external/client-facing writing from SM Agent.
Tone: Professional but warm. No jargon. Concise. Every sentence earns its place.

---

**Subject:** Your automation setup — quick update

Hi [Name],

Just wanted to give you a quick heads-up on where things stand.

We've mapped out your onboarding flow and the data pipeline is taking shape. A few things to confirm before we move forward — I'll send those over in a separate note so nothing gets lost.

You're on track for the timeline we discussed. I'll check in again [specific day] with a progress snapshot.

Anything urgent on your end, just reply here.

Sakina
SM Agent

---

## What makes this work

- Opens with the point — no warm-up filler
- One idea per paragraph
- Gives the client confidence without overpromising
- Ends with a clear next step and an open door
- Warm without being chatty
