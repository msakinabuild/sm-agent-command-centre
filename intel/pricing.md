## Standard Offers

### 1. Automation Audit — AED 1,500

**What it is:** A focused review of how the client currently runs their operations, what's manual, what's breaking, and what should be automated first.

**What's included:**
- 60-minute discovery session
- Written audit report: current state, 3–5 prioritised findings, recommended action plan
- Delivered as a Google Doc within 3 business days

**Best for:** Clients who aren't sure where to start. Great entry point — builds trust before a larger commitment.

**Note:** AED 1,500 is credited toward any package booked within 30 days.

---

### 2. Starter — AED 3,500 (one-time)

**What it is:** One workflow automated. Scoped, built, and handed over.

**What's included:**
- Discovery session to define the workflow
- One workflow built and tested (choose: invoicing, client onboarding, email follow-ups, lead tracking, or similar)
- Handover session + documentation
- 2 weeks of support after delivery

**Best for:** Businesses with one clear pain point who want a quick win before committing to more.

**Timeline:** Delivered within 2 weeks of kickoff.

---

### 3. Growth — AED 8,500 setup + AED 1,500/month

**What it is:** Three workflows automated, an AI assistant configured for the business, and ongoing monthly support.

**Setup includes:**
- Automation audit to identify the best 3 workflows
- Three workflows built and tested
- AI assistant configured (answers questions, drafts emails, runs briefings)
- Handover session + team walkthrough

**Monthly retainer includes:**
- Monitoring and maintenance
- One new workflow or update per quarter
- Priority support (48-hour response)

**Best for:** Growing SMEs who want to automate their core operations and keep building over time.

**Timeline:** Setup delivered within 4 weeks of kickoff.

---

### 4. Full Operations — AED 18,000 setup + AED 3,000/month

**What it is:** A full AI operations build — audit, up to 6 workflows, AI assistant, team training, and ongoing management.

**Setup includes:**
- Full operations audit
- Up to 6 workflows built and tested
- AI personal assistant configured
- Team training session (up to 2 hours)
- Full documentation and handover

**Monthly retainer includes:**
- Full monitoring and management
- Up to 2 new workflows or updates per month
- Priority support (24-hour response)
- Monthly check-in call

**Best for:** Businesses ready to run on AI — want everything set up properly and maintained.

**Timeline:** Setup delivered within 6 weeks of kickoff.

---

### 5. Custom / Bespoke — From AED 12,000

For complex requirements, larger teams, or projects that don't fit a standard package. Quoted after discovery.

Examples: multi-system integrations, industry-specific automations, SaaS builds, data pipelines.
